Editorial Reflection

As the sun dips below the historic skyline of Hemisfair, La Semana Alegre emerges not merely as an event, but as a cultural pilgrimage. This iteration focuses on the "Kinetic Forest" theme—a dialogue between urban architecture and the resilient spirit of the local landscape.

Expect an evening where the sounds of the city blend with curated acoustic sets, and the air carries the scent of cedar and roasted agave. It is a celebration of the present moment, held in the cradle of San Antonio's heritage.
