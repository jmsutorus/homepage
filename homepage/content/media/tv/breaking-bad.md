# Breaking Bad

One of the greatest television dramas ever made. The transformation of Walter White from high school chemistry teacher to drug kingpin is masterfully told across 5 seasons.

## Story Arc
Walter White's descent into darkness is gradual and believable. Each season raises the stakes and shows more of Walt's true nature. The character development is phenomenal for everyone in the cast.

## Favorite Elements
- Bryan Cranston's Emmy-winning performance
- Cinematography with unique camera angles and color symbolism
- Jesse Pinkman's tragic character arc
- The meticulous attention to detail in every episode
- Incredible tension building

## Memorable Episodes
- "Ozymandias" (S5E14) - arguably the best episode of TV ever
- "Face Off" (S4E13) - explosive season finale
- "Felina" (S5E16) - perfect series conclusion

## Verdict
A masterpiece of television storytelling. Every episode matters and builds toward the inevitable conclusion. The writing, acting, and direction are all top-tier.

Highly recommend for anyone who hasn't watched it yet.
