# Inception

Christopher Nolan's mind-bending heist thriller that takes place across multiple dream layers. A masterclass in storytelling and visual effects.

## Plot
Dom Cobb and his team attempt to plant an idea in someone's subconscious through shared dreaming. The complexity of navigating multiple dream levels while being chased by projections makes for intense viewing.

## Best Parts
- The rotating hallway fight scene is legendary
- Hans Zimmer's score perfectly builds tension
- The ambiguous ending sparks endless debates
- Leonardo DiCaprio's emotional performance

## Rating
One of the best sci-fi films of the 2010s. The concept is executed brilliantly and rewards multiple viewings as you catch new details each time.
